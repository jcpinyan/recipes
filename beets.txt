Ingredients:
 - 4 medium beets (2" diameter)

Directions:
 1. Cut off the leaves, but leave about an inch of stems.
 2. Scrub beets well.
 3. Place beets in about a centimeter of water in a microwave-safe dish with a
lid (corningware recommended).
 4. Microwave for 4-5 minutes per beet.
 5. Test for doneness with fork; it should be able to stab in easily.
 6. Fill dish with cold water in sink and let sit until cool enough to handle.
 7. Peels should come away easily by hand in the water.
